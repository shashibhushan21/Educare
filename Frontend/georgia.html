Georgia Climate Overview

Explore the diverse seasons of the Peach State

Spring

March to May brings mild and pleasant weather with blooming flowers and greenery.

Average: 55°F - 75°F (13°C - 24°C)

Perfect for outdoor activities and enjoying Georgia's famous peach blossoms.

Summer

June to August is hot and humid with occasional thunderstorms.

Average: 75°F - 92°F (24°C - 33°C)

Ideal for visiting beaches and water-based recreation.

Fall

September to November offers cooler temperatures and beautiful foliage.

Average: 55°F - 75°F (13°C - 24°C)

Great time for hiking in North Georgia mountains.

Winter

December to February is mild with rare snowfall in the south.

Average: 35°F - 55°F (2°C - 13°C)

Occasional snow possible in northern regions.
